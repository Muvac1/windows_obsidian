- [[变限积分的求导法则]]如果 $G(x) = \int_{a(x)}^{b(x)} f(x,t) dt$，则
    $G'(x) = f(x, b(x)) \cdot b'(x) - f(x, a(x)) \cdot a'(x) + \int_{a(x)}^{b(x)} \frac{\partial}{\partial x} f(x,t) dt$
